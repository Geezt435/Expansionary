### Details

Also known as **Eagle Eye**, this hitman has the composure to kill those far enough away to be unseen. If you think you could get close the gaps to him, look up! His trained vulture disturbs anyone getting nearby, distracting them long enough for the sniper to shoot one's head off clean, so keep in mind to not stay far from him!
