### Details

Mostly known as "Snakeeye", and it really shows why! One better watch where they walk, She rattles out a special gift with high velocity, a living rattlesnake as your pain provider!

---

### Capabilities

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Main Attack">

#### Rattlelauncher

A hefty launcher that shoots a rattlesnake, which can inflict **Muscle Stiff Viper** to enemies, which jump and shoot constantly. **10** seconds cooldown.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Muscle Stiff Viper

Poisonous effect that deals low damage over time but slows the target.

</Tab>

</Tabs>

### Trivia

<Accordion client:load title="Name Change?">

Yes, she is originally a unit named "Snakeeye", and it was a rifle before it become a full blown launcher. Name are changed because it fits more to the **Wild West** aspect.

</Accordion>

<Accordion client:load title="Is the rattlesnake a reference to something?">

If you haven't noticed by the hat or the sharp eye, it is indeed a homage reference to **Rattlesnake Jake** from **Rango**, in development he is supposed to be kinda strong since he would shoot too rapidly considering its a machine gun, but it is nerfed.

</Accordion>
