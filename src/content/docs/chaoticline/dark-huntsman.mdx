### Details

> Beware of the blade that prevail amongst the guns, for it triumph within its own reach.

In the darkest corner of this wasteland, a wrathful hitman lurks in darkness, with sole duty to terminate those who crossed the **Dead Line**. Always seen working alone due to him being extremely aggressive and uncooperative behavior.

<Tab name="Main Attack">

#### Wrathful Sawblade

A brutal and devastating blade with saw teeth attached for separating flesh and metal like a paper. A weapon befitting of a madman, a murderer, or both. Mark victim with **Mutilation** effect.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Mutilation

An instant effect that permanently scar the unit, reducing their max health, which disables further healing. When target health is below **200** HP, it will chop both of target's arms, effectively disarming their weapon. But, if the health is below **50** HP then the decapitation would be directed towards head, instantly killing them.

</Tab>
