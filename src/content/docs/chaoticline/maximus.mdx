### Details

A former mercenary, train conductor, and weapon specialist, this veteran went through a lot to become a legend. A great commander of a war troops in the **Dead Line**. Every weapon and armors he used are made from the fine blueprint he kept to himself from past experiences, made with specialized alloy and extreme fire-power. His capacity in technology and combat are quite formidable.

One cannot simply run away and hide from this calamity!

### Capabilities

<Tab name="Main Attack">

#### Furnace Piston Fist

> Сила солнца и скорость поезда!

A mechanized exoskeleton power-fist with gun attached to it, slight misuse could harm the user if not handled properly, thus it required expertize.

On **melee**, it caused devastating shot that instantly burned the enemy like a metal being forged with heat-blast. On **ranged**, the shot tracks the target, arranging itself towards them. If the bullet hits the target, it marks them, allowing the caster to track and chase them relentlessly if they're still alive. **1** seconds cooldown on melee attacks, **4** seconds cooldown on ranged attacks.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Ability">

#### Hookgear Machinery

Ability to drop a heavy plated hook machine that pulls enemies in large vicinity to it, After **7** seconds, it detonates. **10** seconds cooldown.

#### High Octane Express

> *Никто не может избежать солнца!*

An ultimate ability which upon activation, charges up, then charges towards the furthest enemy position. Upon reaching the destination, the user jumps up and then slams downward, causing a nuke explosion that damage everyone in huge radius and inflicts them with **Heat Radiation**! **50** seconds cooldown.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Heat Radiation

A terrifying boiling effect that causes damage over time, the faster the unit moves, the more damage it takes. Last for **6** seconds.

</Tab>
