### Details

Some men you just can't reach, some capable to dig deep into the core of the earth itself with extreme crushing force, and oh, before he went, he also left a bunch of Bad-O'-Dynamite, a barrage of an explosive, such a heartwarming gift! (And quite a dangerous one if I do say so myself)
