### Details

Carrying an exceedingly heavy shooter, he troubles himself carrying this beast of a cannon that shoots out a **Mega Rumbling Bomb**! This specially crafted bomb type carries its heat-tempered momentum into a rumbling blast wall of an ember splash and an ouch!
