### Details

In times of war, it is comforting to have an ally that cares for your wound. Although of course, all of it ultimately serves the purpose of ensuring the demise of one's enemy.

---

### Capabilities

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Main Attacks">

#### Health Stimpacks Shooter

A simple shooter gun with healing syringe on it, anyone hit will be given the effect of **Healing Compound V3**. **10** seconds cooldown with **2** rounds of magazine.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Healing Compound V3

Third generation of an improved chemical compound to heal wounded allies. Heals **35** health points per second. Now also provides a more potent healing boost, doubles healing when below **25%** health. Last for **7** seconds.

</Tab>

<Tab name="On Death">

#### Medkit Support

Upon death, drops a medical kit that completely heals and provide damage immunity for **2** seconds to any teammates that picks it up.

</Tab>

</Tabs>
