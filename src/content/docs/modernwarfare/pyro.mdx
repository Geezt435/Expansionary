### Details

Burning and soaring flames, wall of bright radiating fire, the field of black ashes and dust remain. Burn them, burn them all to the ground, **paint 'em all in black.**

---

### Capabilities

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Main Attacks">

#### Military Grade Flamethrower

A high-powered flamethrower designed for military use, capable of projecting a stream of burning fuel at enemies. it fires oil projectile that linger for **6** seconds on ground, which constantly deals damage to enemies near it while also inflicting **Scorching Burn**. **20** seconds cooldown.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Scorching Burn

A horrifying burn effect that deals high damage over time and can spread rapidly. Last for **5** seconds.

</Tab>

<Tab name="On Death">

#### Gas Leak Death

On death, explodes, leaving behind a lingering explosion for **6** seconds.

</Tab>
