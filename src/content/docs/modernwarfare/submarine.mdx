### Details

Something appears from the deep water, breaking the surface and suddenly, the next thing you'll see is a barrage of explosions! This war-machine is designed to sustain underwater pressure and even inland, able to deliver a devastating underwater and surface assault! Though it is pretty much immobile and fragile to compensate for such feats.
