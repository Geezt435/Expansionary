### Details

A wraith deity of forest tasked for ecological restoration, capable of ejecting highly condensed acidic fluid, turning those in its way into a sludge waste!

---

### Capabilities

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Main Attack">

#### Biogenic Acid

Nature's strong acid droplets that leaves a puddle of itself, which lingers for **3** seconds. Weapon cooldown is **6** seconds.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Acidic

Effect base when enemies are below **10%** of max health or dead, they instantly melts unit body, killing them immediately. If not, deals damage over time.

</Tab>

<Tab name="On Death">

#### Flora Forfend

On death, Druid explodes into a large blooming acid fumes that confuses enemy on the battlefield.

</Tab>
