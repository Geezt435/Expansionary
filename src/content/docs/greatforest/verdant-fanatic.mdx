### Details

Sealed champion of the **Great Forest**, lightweight, swift, and experienced. Despite its peaceful and soothing demeanor, he's also known as **Green Demon** for a reason.

---

### Capabilities

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Main Attack">

#### Flourished Lance

A long lance that can be used to attack enemies from a distance. On hit, it deals damage and applies **Grove Toxin** effect.

#### Prickly Shield

A pulsating thorny wooden shield. On hit deals damage and applies **Ensnaring Ivy** effect.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Passive">

#### Leaf Steps

Passive ability to surround its target while in air, moving like leaves, circling around them for opportunity to strike.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Ability">

#### Thorns Ensnare

Ability to spawn a fast thorns that pierces enemies caught within, dealing damage and causing **Ensnaring Ivy** effect. **20** - **30** seconds cooldown.

#### Flowgrown Strike

Ability to rotate towards target, then do an instantaneous powerful strike that can be used to attack multiple enemies. On hit, it deals damage and applies **Grove Toxin** effect. **7** seconds cooldown.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Grove Toxin

A toxic effect that deals damage over time and incapacitate target. Last for **10** seconds.

#### Ensnaring Ivy

Effect that ensnares target, dealing damage over time for **5** seconds. Can also damage nearby enemies if they are within range.

</Tab>

</Tabs>

### Trivia

<Accordion client:load title="Fanatic?">

Yes, he is a obsessed with anything from nature, especially forest, living freely in its wilderness. He is a champion that fights for the sake of nature and its preservation. One of his most known achievements is the **Verdant Pact**, a victorious war that results treaty between the Great Forest and the human world, allowing both to coexist peacefully within a distance.

</Accordion>

<Accordion client:load title="Species?">

A **Verdunt**, not fully dryad but a hybrid of dryad and human, born from the fusion of a human and a tree spirit. This makes him one with nature but no limitations of a dryad, allowing him to move freely in the human world. He still prefer to stay in the forest though, where he can feel the most at home.

</Accordion>
