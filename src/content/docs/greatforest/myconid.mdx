### Details

Resident of an underground fungi kingdom, having a truce with the **Elves** for territory.

They don't have a strong body, but upon death, they burst out their sappy parasitic spores, that latched on enemies near it, changing their alliance!

---

### Capabilities

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Main Attack">

#### Sap Hand

A sticky hand that makes enemies sticks to the hand, deals low damage, but keep enemy in place.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Myconia Fungus

Effect that instantly deals **100** damage, rebirth the victim if they're dead into a *Myconid* within the next **4** seconds, which is a lesser health version of themself with **10** seconds expiration health. On this mode, when they dies once again, only summons a shroom explosion that doesn't causes **Myconia Fungus** effect but disables attack for **2** seconds.

</Tab>

<Tab name="On Death">

#### Spore Blast

On death, summons a spore that damages nearby enemies, deals **100** damage, and infects them with **Myconia Fungus**.

</Tab>

</Tabs>
