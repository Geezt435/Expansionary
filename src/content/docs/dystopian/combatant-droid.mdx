### Details

A robot designed generally to handle riots. Using a metal shield, it bashed and pushed away opposition forcefully.

### Capabilities

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Main Attacks">

#### Riot Metal Shield

A metal shield great for the use of holding back riots.

#### Tazing Baton

An electric metal baton, inflicts the **Tazed** effect.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Tazed

A status effect that temporarily paralyzes the target, preventing them from taking any actions. Last for **2** seconds.

</Tab>

<Tab name="On Death">

#### Self Destruct Sequence

Upon malfunction or expiration, sets down a timed explosive that detonates in **3** seconds.

</Tab>
