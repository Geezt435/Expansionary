### Details

An orphan with absurd intelligence, a prodigy of remote control-based technology, both fueled with deep yearning for proof of excellence and need of protection, she used her own-made puppets that resemble those who once abandons her.

#### Snaring Strings

> It connects to the stage, only the fool know the path of no harm.

#### Android Puppetry

*Puppeteer* controls **2** regenerative parent doll that instantly deactivated when she dies. One being ranged and one being melee.

<Tab name="Passives">

#### Puppet Body

All damage is reduced by **50%**.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Wrung

A two-stage status effect.

*First Application:*
Reduces the target's movement speed for **4** seconds.

*Second Application:*
If applied again while the first stage is active, it is upgraded to a stronger effect. The target is stunned and all attack and abilities cooldown are reset. Last for **2** seconds.

</Tab>
