### Details

A genetically improved wobbler-beast hybrid, equipped in steel armor that almost resembles a dragon armor. He wields a beam-enhanced longsword, it's very destructive so don't touch the edges!

### Capabilities

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Main Attacks">

#### Charged Positron

> It connects to the stage, only the fool know the path of no harm.

A guaranteed hit attack that uses the strings. Inflicts **Wrung** effect. **4** seconds cooldown.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Abilities">

#### Electron Conduit

He charges his bat, making the next swing to deal double damage to enemies and flinging the target upward.

#### Combo Slash / Holographic Defecto

Ultimate ability to execute **20** consecutive slices against chosen targets in range, bringing them up for a final slash on top.

</Tab>

#### Antivirus

</Tab>

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Wrung

A two-stage status effect.

*First Application:*
Reduces the target's movement speed for **4** seconds.

*Second Application:*
If applied again while the first stage is active, it is upgraded to a stronger effect. The target is stunned and all attack and abilities cooldown are reset. Last for **2** seconds.

</Tab>
