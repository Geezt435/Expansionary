### Details

Being a fisher hobbyist, this old folk can reel in something bigger than his own body weight. Better watch out, lest you end up baited like a fish!

#### Fishing Rod

A long fishing rod that can be used to reel in enemies, act both as ranged and melee weapon. When fishing, it can be used to reel in enemies and pull them closer, he also dashes back.
