### Details

> A human? A corpse? _Is it even a living being?_

Seemingly an animated doll made out of _tropical woods_, it have a highly pungent smell of _balsamic oil_. Its unsettlingly fast movement and mischievous expression indirectly tells you its intentions are not so... friendly.

---

### Capabilities

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Main Attack">

#### Sunlight Keris

A blade attack that inflict damage even on touch. It has a chance to inflict **Blazeshine** effect on the target.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Passive">

#### Twilight Sunlight

Passive movement ability to transcend reality, blinking for every few nanosecond and making every collision ablaze any unit it touches, dealing few damage.

#### Sunshine Light

Simple passive light that illuminates the surrounding area, giving a small amount of light to the area around it.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Blazeshine

A dangerous effect that can be inflicted high damage per second, it last for **6** seconds. It can spread to nearby units in near radius.

</Tab>

<Tab name="On Death">

#### Sunlight Burst

When the unit dies, after **1** second delay, it will explode into a very bright and forceful explosion, causing **Blazeshine** effect to nearby units.

</Tab>

</Tabs>
