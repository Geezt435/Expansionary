### Details

> Legend told it was once a statue of a certain **Dewa**, having its parts removed to cover its true identity.

Strange mystical statue that admires anyone around it, giving them a sense of rush. They're useless without followers.

#### Tropical Blessing

Drastically increased allies attack speed for every **7** seconds in a very high radius. The **Totem** will not attack and acts as a support unit.

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Tropical Blessing

A short-timed buff that increases allies attack speed by **150** points that decays over time.

#### Tropical Blessing Indicator

Simply an indicator that shows units when near the **Totem** are in vacinity to be blessed with **Tropical Blessing** effect.

</Tab>

#### Crumble

When the **Totem** dies, it will crumble into pieces.
