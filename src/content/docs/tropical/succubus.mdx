### Details

> Better brings fewer friends, or ended up betrayed shamelessly!

**Kiss**! And now her enemy becomes an ally! Blessed with incomparable beauty, she lures enemies to switch sides!

### Capabilities

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Main Attack">

#### Chant Heart Fist

A melee attack that deals small damage but causes **Cupid** *AoE* effect, which attract enemies in.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Ability">

#### Kiss

Ability to lean head on target head, dealing low damage but incapacitates the target.

#### Tempt

Temp enemy in near vacinity to switch sides permanently. Has a **18** seconds cooldown. On close range, **12** seconds cooldown.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Cupid

[Cupid Effect](https://totally-accurate-battle-simulator.fandom.com/wiki/Heart_Bow)

#### Temp Enemy

An instant effect that makes the target switch sides. Has **50%** chances of success and only if the target is alive.

</Tab>
