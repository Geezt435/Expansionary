### Details

Moves sinuously, honed and born to hunt. Unlike their offspring, adept enough to avoid self-infliction or harming their hunting packs!

---

#### Predatory

**Attack** won't damage itself or teammates, causes damage upon intact. It can also attack using its tail.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Passive">

#### Corpse Devour

Upon contact with a dead body, it will **eat** the corpse, healing itself for **25%** of the corpse's max HP.
