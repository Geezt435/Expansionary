### Details

Moves sinuously, honed and born to hunt. Unlike their offspring, adept enough to avoid self-infliction or harming their hunting packs!

---

### Capabilities

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Main Attack">

**Attack** with bite, won't damage itself or teammates, causes damage upon intact. Bites sticks victim to its head.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Passive">

#### Corpse Devour

Upon head contact with a dead body, it will **eat** the corpse, healing itself for **25%** or increase its own max health by **25%** of the corpse's max HP. It even eats its own ally!

</Tab>
