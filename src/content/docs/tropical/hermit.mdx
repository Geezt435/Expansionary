### Details

A beach-dwelling nomad, often bedding down along the shore, riding the rhythms of the waves. They seek refuge in their portable barrel at the first sign of danger, briefly becoming invulnerable.

---

### Capabilities

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Main Attack">

#### Stick Throw

The Hermit throws **3** sticks that deals small damage. It have a **6** seconds cooldown.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Ability">

#### Barrel Hide

When the enemy is near, he will hide in the barrel, becoming immune momentarily. After **5** seconds, pops back out.
