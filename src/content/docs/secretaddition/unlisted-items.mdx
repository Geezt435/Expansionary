### Capabilities

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Abilities">

#### On Vehicle Death Summon Rider

When the vehicle dies, deploys the rider. Rider is stored upon initiation and deployed when the vehicle is destroyed.

#### Always Healthbar

Always enables the health bar regardless of the settings.

#### No Healthbar

Disables health bar regardless of the settings.

#### Keep Attacking

Make unit attacks algorithm simplified: If there's target, then attack. If on possession, completely ignored.

#### Keep Standing

Used by mostly all melee fighters, it kept the unit from wobbling and stumbling too much, keeping them on their feet.

#### Attack Give Targeting

Give a custom red targeting effect when attacking a target. Used mostly by the **Chaotic Line** faction.

#### Titanium Plated Kevlar

A reinforced armor plating that provides enhanced protection against ballistic and impact damage. All bullet damage is reduced by **50%**. Used by **Modern Warfare** faction, but only on humanoid units.

#### Water Affinity

A passive ability that makes unit able to live on water, even heals some of their health from it.
