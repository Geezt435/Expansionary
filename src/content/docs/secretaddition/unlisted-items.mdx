### Capabilities

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Abilities">

#### Locked Legs

Make both legs and knee unable to rotate.

#### On Vehicle Death Summon Rider

When the vehicle dies, deploys the rider. Rider is stored upon initiation and deployed when the vehicle is destroyed.

#### Always Healthbar

Always enables the health bar regardless of the settings.

#### No Healthbar

Disables health bar regardless of the settings.

#### Keep Attacking

Make unit attacks algorithm simplified: If there's target, then attack. If on possession, completely ignored.

#### Keep Standing

Used by mostly all melee fighters, it kept the unit from wobbling and stumbling too much, keeping them on their feet.

#### Attack Give Targeting

Give a custom red targeting effect when attacking a target. Used mostly by the **Chaotic Line** faction.

#### Titanium Plated Kevlar

A reinforced armor plating that provides enhanced protection against ballistic and impact damage. All bullet damage is reduced by **50%**. Used by **Modern Warfare** faction, but only on humanoid units.

#### Water Affinity

A passive ability that makes unit able to live on water, even heals some of their health from it.

#### Monochrome Overlay

Give the unit a circular overlay that makes anything around them to be monochrome, except for glowing materials. Mainly used by **Retropolis** faction.

#### Monochrome Reverse Overlay

Same with **Monochrome Overlay** but reversed. Used by **Stereo**'s **Doppelganger**.

#### Noir Inner Overlay

The inner light overlay for **Noir**. They say it represents the light of truth in a world of shadows.

#### Noir Outer Overlay

The outer dark overlay for **Noir**. They say it represents the darkness that hides the truth in a world of light.

#### Insanity Overlay

The overlay used by **Ringmaster**. When health is below **50%**, changed the visual appearance to a darker tone.

#### Synthetic Robotic Forging

A passive ability that reduces damage by the ratio of max health to current health, with a maximum damage reduction of **80%**. Used mostly by **Dystopian** faction, which is **Combatant Droid, Dusk Platoon, and Ionic Tyrant**.
