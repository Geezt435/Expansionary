### Details

Local champ of chomping, with the powerful clamp of a metal bear trap, horrendously and dangerously shut and reopens itself, enough to rip your leg, as if it was a jaw of a beast!

---

### Capabilities

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Main Attack">

#### Chomptrap Fist

A dangerous metal trap fist that can clamp down on enemies, dealing significant damage activation.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Ability">

#### Bear Trap Setup

Ability to throw a few setup bear trap that will activate when an enemy steps on it, dealing damage and dealing **Deepleg Wound** effect to the victim. **8 - 10** seconds cooldown. The trap will last for **30** seconds before it removes itself.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Deepleg Wound

Victim will suffer from a deep leg wound which will cause them to bleed and take damage over time. They would move drastically slower, making it harder for them to escape or engage. Last for **10** seconds.

</Tab>

</Tabs>

<Accordion client:load title="What's Chompion?">

A combination of a chomper and a champion, it simply means a champion of chomping. It is a play on words, as it is a character that is both a champion and a chomper.
