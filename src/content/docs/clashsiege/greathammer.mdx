### Details

A strong yet doofus soldier, but intelligence doesn't matter when you have a giant hammer to smash everything in your way!

Carrying a hefty hammer, he quake a tremor wherever its landed! It's so powerful that even heavy enemies would be pushed back by its shockwave!

#### Trampling Hammer

A giant hammer forged in hard and solid metal. So heavy that it has a whopping **12** seconds cooldown! It goes faster if it land a hit though, so better aim carefully. When landed a hit, it would summon a shockwave that pushes even heavy enemies! Direct hit would cause the unit to be **Dizzy**.

#### Dizzy

Effect that causes victim concussion, unable to attack and walk momentarily.
