### Details

A crazed sleepless magician that learned the power of pure force magic, capable of controlling physics in combat. His wand is strong enough, but there's one more trick up his slick wizard sleeve, so beware!

---

### Capabilities

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Main Attack">

#### Kinetic Magic Wand

A short magic wand that throws out raw compressed magic, dealing damage to enemies. IF enemy is hit, they will gain a **Kinetic Magic** effect.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Passive">

#### Effect Immunity

Immunity from all non-instant effects **(Poison, Fire, ext)**. Removed effect are converted into a static **25** point heal.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Kinetic Magic

Effect that randomly pushes and pulls enemies in random directions, causing them to be disoriented and unable to attack or move effectively, which also damages them for **50** point every **2** seconds. Last for **10** seconds.

</Tab>

</Tabs>
