### Details

Fearless, these warriors trained with a longbow and hunts in packs.

They chase their prey relentlessly with their poisonous arrows, causing lethal shots!

---

### Capabilities

<Tab name="Main Attack">

#### Vital Poison Longbow

A flexible and tall wooden longbow, they shot a poisonous arrow that lethally incapacitate their victim with **Vital Poison**. When near his target, they will increase arrow shots from just **1** to **3** arrows.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Vital Poison

An enhanced poison effect that disabled attack and disables movement momentarily.
