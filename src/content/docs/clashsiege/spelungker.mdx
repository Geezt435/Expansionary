### Details

A once former tactician delves into the battlefield, equipped with an automated bronze drill bore, dealing heavy demolition to the opposing force with its ingenious contrivances. Don't think the back is safe just because its behind an army!

---

### Capabilities

<Tab name="Main Attack">

#### Steampunk Drill

A powerful drill bore that can be used to attack enemies in front of him, dealing heavy damage and knocking them back. Very dangerous in close range due to its capability in dealing damage in contact.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Ability">

#### Burrow

A chase ability with medium cooldown to burrow underground, leaving an areal ground damage wherever he goes and slowly goes to the furthest enemy last position and resurfaces back to the ground.
