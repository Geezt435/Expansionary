### Details

An enthusiast with knack for upgrading almost everything in her sight to even more pristine condition! Her custom steampunk mechanic wrench are told to be handmade by a certain engineer, and always at ready for some smacking!

### Capabilities

#### Cog Wrench

A mechanized wrench handy for tuning up a cog machinery, somehow it always fits perfectly.

<Tab name="Abilities">

#### Tinkering Upgrade

One-time ability that instantly starts on battle to buff teammates with **Upgrade**, if no teammates are found, she upgrades herself instead.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Upgrade

Tinker teammates attack and walking speed, upgrading both status by double. Last permanently until death.
