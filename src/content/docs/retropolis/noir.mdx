### Details

The mysterious dark and shady detective of a city that never sleeps, possessing keen and gifted senses for gunfights. Despite employing a firearm, don't underestimate him in close-range combat! His mobility and tenacity will suddenly burst forth, leaving nothing but cloth filled with holes.

He takes his job very seriously and is always prepared with additional guns and bullet resources on his side, always has been and always will be, unafraid to be more cruel than criminals.
