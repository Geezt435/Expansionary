### Details

Ahh young spirit, rebellious and energetic! So much to explore, and with the help of this roller shoe, she can traverse the battlefield with unmatched agility!

### Capabilities

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Main Attacks">

#### Paint Canister

A canned paint canister used for attacking enemies at mid range. Can pierce through multiple enemies, deals low damage but fast rate.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Abilities">

#### Vintagia Phonostylus

Ability to charge up a tornado for a period of time, after a delay, creates a large temporary tornado that stays in place that disturbs projectiles and enemies upward, dealing a bit of damage in that area! The tornado stays for **5** seconds, ability has **20** Seconds cooldown.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Passives">

#### Roller Movement

Passive ability that gives custom legs movement and slight upward trajectory from ground.

</Tab>
