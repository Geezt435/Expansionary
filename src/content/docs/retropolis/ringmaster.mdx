### Details

All black, all white. Spades and hearts. A King, a Queen, and their colorful Jokers. Laughter swept through the courtyard before giving way to an uneasy silence, broken only by an even louder burst of manic laughter. The King fell, and so did his Queen, yet the Jester and the Mime remain, quietly pulling the strings as the curtains refuse to close.

### Capabilities

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Main Attacks">

#### Snaring Strings

> It connects to the stage, only the fool know the path of no harm.

A guaranteed hit attack that uses the strings. Inflicts **Wrung** effect. **4** seconds cooldown.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Abilities">

#### Carnival Twin

One-time ability to summon the **Ringmaster**'s twin. While summoned, if the main unit still alive, the twin is completely invulnerable. In addition if health is below **50%**, increase attack speed for both unit.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Passives">

#### Acrobatic Athletic

Unique movement ability to perform air maneuvers. Making them hard to hit.

#### Freakshow

Passive to inflict a total of alteration from everyone in a large area, then every **5** seconds, spawn a curtain that heals allies from that total and damage enemies from that total. Alteration total calculation come from total missing health divided by total units.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Wrung

A two-stage status effect.

*First Application:*
Reduces the target's movement speed for **4** seconds.

*Second Application:*
If applied again while the first stage is active, it is upgraded to a stronger effect. The target is stunned and all attack and abilities cooldown are reset. Last for **2** seconds.

</Tab>

</Tabs>
