### Details

One lone TV, flickering amidst the darkness, inside figure runs closer, sprinting like it's chasing the screen...

But at the end, **BAM**! A loud static noise fills the air. The TV switched off and everything seems back to normal, but you're hearing two audios at once! Everything becomes blurry, you stared at the window, and it all now makes sense. It sounds like it's time for a good ol' purge, doesn't it?
