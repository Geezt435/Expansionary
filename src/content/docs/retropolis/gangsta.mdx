### Details

A street thug rampant with street fighting style, wielding a pipe, and sporting a crazy hairstyle. **DO NOT RIDICULE HIS HAIR**.

### Capabilities

<Tabs client:load>

<Tab name="Main Attacks">

#### Industrial Long Pipe

A long pipe scrapped from a factory used as a weapon, effective at close range. Attacked enemies get affected with **Whacked**.

</Tab>

<Tab name="Effects">

#### Whacked

De-buff enemies, resetting all attack counter to **0**, while also marking them unable to attack for **1** seconds.

</Tab>

</Tabs>
